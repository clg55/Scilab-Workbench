Scilab Function

NAME

    stripblanks - strips leading and trailing blanks of strings

CALLING SEQUENCE

txt=stripblanks(txt)

PARAMETERS

    • txt : string or matrix of strings

DESCRIPTION

    stripblanks strips leading and trailing blanks of strings

EXAMPLES

    a='  123   ';
'!'+a+'!'
'!'+stripblanks(a)+'!'
a=['  123   ',' xyz']
strcat(stripblanks(a))
