Scilab Function

NAME

    bezout - Bezout equation for polynomials

CALLING SEQUENCE

[thegcd,U]=bezout(p1,p2)

PARAMETERS

    • p1, p2 : two real polynomials

DESCRIPTION

    computes GCD thegcd of p1 and p2 and in addition a (2x2) unimodular matrix U such that:

    [p1,p2]*U = [thegcd,0]

    The lcm of p1 and p2 is given by:

    p1*U(1,2) (or -p2*U(2,2) )

EXAMPLES

    x=poly(0,'x');
p1=(x+1)*(x-3)^5;p2=(x-2)*(x-3)^3;
[thegcd,U]=bezout(p1,p2) 
det(U)
clean([p1,p2]*U)
thelcm=p1*U(1,2)
lcm([p1,p2])
