Scilab Function

NAME

    polar - polar form

CALLING SEQUENCE

[Ro,Theta]=polar(A)

PARAMETERS

    • A : real or complex square matrix
    • Ro, : real matrix
    • Theta, : real or complex matrix

DESCRIPTION

    returns the polar form of A i.e.:

    A=Ro*expm(%i*Theta) Ro symmetric >=0 and Theta hermitian >=0.

EXAMPLES

    A=rand(5,5);
[Ro,Theta]=polar(A);
norm(A-Ro*expm(%i*Theta),1)
