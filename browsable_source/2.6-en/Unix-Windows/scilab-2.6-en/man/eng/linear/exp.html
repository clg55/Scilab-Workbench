Scilab Function

NAME

    exp - element-wise exponential

CALLING SEQUENCE

exp(X)

PARAMETERS

    • X : scalar,vector or matrix with real or complex entries.

DESCRIPTION

    exp(X) is the (element-wise) exponential of the entries of X .

EXAMPLES

    x=[1,2,3+%i];
log(exp(x))  //element-wise
2^x
exp(x*log(2))
