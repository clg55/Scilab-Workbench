Scilab Function

NAME

    trace - trace

CALLING SEQUENCE

trace(X)

PARAMETERS

    • X : real or complex square matrix, polynomial or rational matrix.

DESCRIPTION

    trace(X) is the trace of the matrix X .

    Same as sum(diag(X)) .

EXAMPLES

    A=rand(3,3);
trace(A)-sum(spec(A))
