Scilab Function

NAME

    intc - Cauchy integral

CALLING SEQUENCE

[y]=intc(a,b,f)

PARAMETERS

    • a,b : two complex numbers
    • f : "external" function

DESCRIPTION

    If f is a complex-valued function, intc(a,b,f) computes the integral from a to b of f(z)dz along the straight line a b of the complex plane.
