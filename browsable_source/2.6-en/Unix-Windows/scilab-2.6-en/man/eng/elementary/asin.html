Scilab Function

NAME

    asin - sine inverse

CALLING SEQUENCE

[t]=asin(x)

PARAMETERS

    • x : real or complex vector/matrix
    • t : real or complex vector/matrix

DESCRIPTION

    The entries of t are sine inverse of the corresponding entries of x . Definition domain is [-1, 1]. It takes his values in sets

EXAMPLES

    A=[1,2;3,4]
sin(asin(A))
