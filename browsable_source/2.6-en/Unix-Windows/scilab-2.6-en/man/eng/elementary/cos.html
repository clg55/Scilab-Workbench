Scilab Function

NAME

    cos - cosine function

CALLING SEQUENCE

[y]=cos(x)

PARAMETERS

    • x : real or complex vector/matrix

DESCRIPTION

    For a vector or a matrix, cos(x) is the cosine of its elements . For matrix cosine use cosm(X) function.

EXAMPLES

    x=[0,1,%i]
acos(cos(x))
