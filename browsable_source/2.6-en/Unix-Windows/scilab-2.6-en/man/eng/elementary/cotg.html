Scilab Function

NAME

    cotg - cotangent

CALLING SEQUENCE

[t]=cotg(x)

PARAMETERS

    • x,t : real or complex vectors/matrices

DESCRIPTION

    The elements of t are the cotangents of the corresponding entries of x . t=cos(x)./sin(x)

EXAMPLES

    x=[1,%i];
cotg(x)-cos(x)./sin(x)
