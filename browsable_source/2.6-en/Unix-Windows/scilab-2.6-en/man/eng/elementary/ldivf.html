Scilab Function

NAME

    ldivf - left symbolic division

CALLING SEQUENCE

ldivf('d','c')

DESCRIPTION

    returns the string 'c\d' Trivial simplifications such as '1\c' = 'c' are performed.

EXAMPLES

    ldivf('1','1')
ldivf('a','0')
ldivf('a','x')
ldivf('2','4')
