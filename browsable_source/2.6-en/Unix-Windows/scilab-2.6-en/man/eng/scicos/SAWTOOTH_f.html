Scicos Block

NAME

    SAWTOOTH_f - Scicos sawtooth wave generator

DESCRIPTION

    This block is a sawtooth wave generator: output is (t-t_i) from ti to t_(i+1) where t_i and t_(i+1) denote the times of two successive input events.

DIALOGUE PARAMETERS

    None.
