Scilab Function

NAME

    equil - balancing of pair of symmetric matrices

CALLING SEQUENCE

T=equil(P,Q)

PARAMETERS

    • P, Q : two positive definite symmetric matrices
    • T : nonsingular matrix

DESCRIPTION

    equil returns t such that:

    T*P*T' and inv(T)'*Q*inv(T) are both equal to a same diagonal and positive matrix.

EXAMPLES

    P=rand(4,4);P=P*P';
Q=rand(4,4);Q=Q*Q';
T=equil(P,Q)
clean(T*P*T')
clean(inv(T)'*Q*inv(T))
