Scilab Function

stripblanks - strips leading and trailing blanks of strings

Sequence d'appel

txt=stripblanks(txt)

Parametres

    • txt : string or matrix of strings

Description

stripblanks strips leading and trailing blanks of strings

Exemples

    a='  123   ';
'!'+a+'!'
'!'+stripblanks(a)+'!'
a=['  123   ',' xyz']
strcat(stripblanks(a))
